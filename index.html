About Us

Abbotts Glass Ltd. is the longest running glass merchants in Chorley with over 45 years' experience.

As a small company who supply and fit a range of glazing products, we pride ourselves on our personal service and no hard sell attitude.

With a wealth of experience and knowledge, we can offer a variety of skills including a bevelling service, double glazed units and in-house sandblasting for glass designs.
